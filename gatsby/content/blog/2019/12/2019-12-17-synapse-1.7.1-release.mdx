Synapse 1.7.1 is a security release and we advise all admins to upgrade asap.

Full details follow, but the most important change improves event authorisation thereby preventing the ability to add certain events to a given room erroneously.

As always, you can get the new update [here](https://github.com/matrix-org/synapse/releases/tag/v1.7.1) or any of the sources mentioned at https://github.com/matrix-org/synapse. Also, check out our Synapse installation guide page
