SchildiChat Web/Desktop is a fork of Element Web/Desktop with a more traditional messaging  experience.
You can use the web version at https://app.schildi.chat/, read more at https://schildi.chat.

There is also a [desktop version](https://schildi.chat/desktop/) and a [mobile app](https://schildi.chat/android/) (Android).
Contributions are also very welcome, be it in the form of code, bug reports or translations.
